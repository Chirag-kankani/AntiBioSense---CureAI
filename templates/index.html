About AntibioSense

"Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) kills 1.27 million people per year worldwide and is projected to cause 10 million deaths annually by 2050." — WHO

How It Works
1

LLM Reasoning

Clinical symptoms are analyzed using Groq's LLaMA 3.3 70B to infer the most probable bacterial pathogen instantly.

2

ML Prediction

15 independent Random Forest models analyze patient risk factors against historical resistance data to predict susceptibility.

3

Genomic Context

Predictions are enriched with verified gene associations from the Comprehensive Antibiotic Resistance Database (CARD).
